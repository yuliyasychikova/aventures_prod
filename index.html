Andrey Kolodyuk

Cofounder, Managing Partner

Andrey Kolodyuk, entrepreneur turned VC, has founded over a dozen companies with total revenues exceeding $1 billion. He built and exited two large retail chains in Ukraine, and driven successful businesses in software, telecommunications and media. Andrey is founder and CEO of Divan.TV, an OTT TV service provider with 300 million audience of Russian speakers.

Andrey holds a PhD in Law from Koretsky Institute of State and Law (Ukraine), and Bachelor degrees from Kyiv Politechnical Institute (Ukraine), and Kyiv National Taras Shevchenko University (Ukraine). Andrey has been active in building the entrepreneurial community in Ukraine and Russia for decades and is the Chairman of UVCA - Ukrainian Venture Capital and Private Equity Association. For his accomplishments, Andrey was recognized as a Young Global Leader by the World Economic Forum in 2008. He is fluent in English, Russian, and Ukrainian.

Yevhen Sysoyev

Cofounder, Managing Partner

Yevgen Sysoyev, a former successful investment banker, co-founded AVentures. Over the years, he’s been a board director and advisor to a number of his portfolio companies. He is recognized as the one of most influential people in Ukrainian venture community.

Yevgen holds a MS in Mathematics and Economics from Brandenburg University of Technology and MBA from INSEAD. He sits on the Supervisory Board Members of the BrainBasket Foundation, an NGO with a mission to improve the IT education in Ukraine. Yevgen is fluent in English, German, Russian, Ukrainian, and speaks conversational Spanish.

About this Website

This is an unofficial AVentures website. It was created by the Petcube team with participation from other Aventures portfolio companies as a birthday gift to AVentures. Most VCs create their websites themselves. We decided it will be much better if we, the founders, share how it feels to work with AVentures.

Why did we do this? We’ve been working with Yevgen and Andrey for quite some time, and through their incredible support, we’ve achieved continued growth and success. They are incredibly talented, resourceful, and hardworking in the VC community. This website is our tribute, reflecting our gratitude towards AVentures and to serve as an inspiration for aspiring entrepreneurs. Great investors should get credit for being great!
