About

AVentures Capital team backs talented Ukrainian entrepreneurs with innovative ideas, global thinking, and solid execution skills. The fund invests in early stage startups as well as advises IT businesses across all stages and other funds on strategy and execution. They split their time between Kyiv and San Francisco, investing in companies that disrupt global markets while using Ukraine-based R&D teams as a leverage in global competition.

Being entrepreneurs themselves, Yevgen and Andrey commit to building successful businesses out of Ukraine by leveraging their experience and a hands-on approach which makes them great partners for an early stage company.

The fund's investment focus spans across software tech, including such areas as machine learning, big data, AR, VR, SaaS, cloud, storage, enterprise, web, mobile, IoT and others.

Andrey Kolodyuk

Co-founder, Managing Partner

Andrey Kolodyuk, entrepreneur turned VC, started his entrepreneurial career in New York in 1992 and eventually has founded and built over 10 companies in IT, telecom, internet and media with $1 Billion total revenues. Andrey is the founder of Divan.TV, an OTT service provider with 300 million Ukrainian and Russian-speaking audience living worldwide.

Andrey has been active in building the entrepreneurial community in Ukraine and in August 2014 has initiated a foundation of UVCA (Ukrainian Venture Capital and Private Equity Association). Andrey was selected as a Chairman of Association in November, 2014.

For his accomplishments, Andrey was recognized as a Young Global Leader by the World Economic Forum in 2008. Andrey holds a PhD in Law with the specialty “Political Culture and Ideology. He is fluent in English, Russian, and Ukrainian.

Yevgen Sysoyev

Co-founder, Managing Partner

Yevgen Sysoyev, a former successful investment banker, co-founded AVentures. Over the years, he’s been a board director and advisor to a number of his portfolio companies. He is recognized as the one of most influential people in Ukrainian tech community.

Yevgen holds a MS in Mathematics and Economics from Brandenburg University of Technology and MBA from INSEAD. He sits on the Supervisory Board Members of the BrainBasket Foundation, an NGO with a mission to improve the IT education in Ukraine. Yevgen is fluent in English, German, Russian, Ukrainian, and speaks conversational Spanish.

About this Website

This is an unofficial AVentures website. It was created by the Petcube team with participation from other Aventures portfolio companies as a birthday gift to AVentures. Most VCs create their websites themselves. We decided it will be much better if we, the founders, share how it feels to work with AVentures.

Why did we do this? We’ve been working with Yevgen and Andrey for quite some time, and through their incredible support, we’ve achieved continued growth and success. They are incredibly talented, resourceful, and hardworking in the VC community. This website is our tribute, reflecting our gratitude towards AVentures and to serve as an inspiration for aspiring entrepreneurs. Great investors should get credit for being great!
